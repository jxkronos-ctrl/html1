Coquito for the holidays!

Are you tired of the same old Egg nog every year? spice it up for the holidays this year with this delightful Puerto Rican cocktail!

Ingredients Instructions Key-Tips Ingredients Purchase sites

Ingredients

Make sure to have all ingredients before starting!

  • Coco Lopez
  • White Rum
  • Condensed milk
  • Evaporated milk
  • Coconut milk
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions

After you have gathered all the necessary ingredients, you are now ready to put everything together!

  1. Take out your blender and combine the condensed milk, evaporated milk, coconut milk, and coco lopez
  2. Next add in 1 tsp of cinnamon, 1 tsp of nutmeg, and 2 tsp of vanilla extract
  3. Finally, add in 2 1/2 cups of white rum (you can alsways use less if you want a weaker drink)
  4. Fully blend to make sure all ingredients are properly mixed in.
  5. Pour into glass bottles and make sure to seal tightly and refrigerate

Key Tips

Making the perfect Coquito requires avoiding some common first time mistakes!

  1. Make sure to avoid substituting white rum for dark rum if you want to maintain the traditional Coquito flavour! it is better to make the sacrifice to go back out to buy or borrow some white rum.
  2. Make sure that all ingredients are blended completely. It is not uncommon for people to pour out the coquito from the blender prematurely just to find clumps of unmixed coco lopez in what is supposed to be a smooth creamy cocktail!
  3. Make sure to have all necessary food measuring cups and spoons so that you can be precise! Doing so enables consistant results everytime.
